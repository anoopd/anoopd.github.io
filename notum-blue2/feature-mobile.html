Notum For Mobile

The dedicated Business to customer engagement platform where businesses are guaranteed that communications are delivered to their customers, and customers are guaranteed their privacy.

Notum mobile app is designed by a mom with an intention to develop a platform for all business interactions. We had two objectives while building the Notum Mobile app, Your privacy,andyour convenience.

Your Privacy-You are Right

We believe in this age where all the major platforms are pryingfor your personal information,there is a need for a platform which safeguards your privacy. We at Notum are dedicatedto making sure Notum is a safe, private,and secure place for you to connect with the Businesswho matter.

  • When you subscribe to a business in the Notum platform,you have the absolute authority to decide what personal information you wanted to share with that Business. No dataother than the one that you chose to share will be shared with the Business. For,e.g.,If you choose to share only your first name and last name to a business, Notum will share only that information to them. When the Businesssends a message,we will deliver the message to you based on your communication preference set in Notum.
  • We will not track you or sell your personalized information to any third-party business.
  • You have complete control over which Businesscan communicate with you. We provide a single click unsubscribe with which we guarantee that you don’t get any unwanted messages from any of the businesses.
    For,e.g.,you often end up getting lots of unwanted emails from lots of Businessthat you had previously interacted with. Even after hitting that unsubscribe button a million times,you still receive those emails over and over again.

Complete control over which Business can communicate with you

  • Instantly see a list of all your message subscription with businesses.
  • Unsubscribe easily by a click of a button.
  • Notum guarantees that you don’t get any unwanted messages from any of the businesses you unsubscribed.

Your convenience

Have you ever ended up in a situation where you had to search multiple platforms to find a piece of important information or a message? If your answer is Yes, then Notum is your solution. Notum mobile app is your “one-stop solution” where you can view and respond to all the communications that are sent by the businesses who use the Notum platform.

All communications from all the businesses you interact with in one place....

  • Notum mobile app provides you a centralized platform to view and respond to messages sent by Different Businesses to you.
  • Message prioritization assists you not to miss any high priority messages from business.
  • View and Filter the messages based on Message Types, Categories,and Priorities

Custom Call-to Action buttons on Messages

  • Respond to messages with single clock Call-to Action Buttons
  • For example,An event message will have to accept, reject, call back buttons

Tag a business to a family member

  • Create and Tag family members to a subscribed business
  • Quick overview of the schedules of a specific family member without the hassle of going through various messages.

Chat with businesses

  • Easily connect with your favorite businesses to reschedule an appointment, make a reservation, get customer support, find deals,and more.

Sign up to a business with a simple QR Code.

  • Each Business is assigned with a Unique Notum ID
  • You can either use QR Code scanner or Notum App to subscribe to a business.

Organize business with categories

There is an easy way to organize the businesses you interact with in different categories so that it's easy to manage the messages from them.
