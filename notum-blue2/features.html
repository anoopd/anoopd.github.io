Notum was born out of a desire to simplify the communication between businesses and their customers.

We provided small businesses with a complete platform to manage all aspects of the Business from a single portal. Be it managing your contacts, communicating with your customers, sending reminders, or setting up appointments,you can do all of these from a single application. Thus,allowing you to attain customer satisfaction and manage your Business effortlessly.

Manage Contacts and customers

  • Search for your customers and contacts.
  • Import your existing contacts to Notum
  • Arrange your contacts into groups for targeted Communication and easy organization.

Easy Two-way Communication with your customers

  • Choose specific message types for maximum customer engagement.For Example:Reminder, Alert, Promotion,etc.
  • Personalize the messages that you send to your customers.
  • Your messages will be auto formatted and delivered based on your customer’s preferred Communication channel.
  • Notify customers about Appointments periodically to avoid No Shows.
  • Ability to Automate and schedule messages to selected contacts or groups
  • Assign priorities to your messages to indicate their level of importance
  • Get message delivery and read receipts (conditions apply).
  • Customers can sign up for your Business with simple steps.

Scheduling appointments and events

  • Ability to set up different types of events with your customers
  • Ability to assign your staff to events.
  • Ability view and manage all events from the portal
  • Receive RSVPs and know in advance who’s attending your events
  • Notum will send automated reminders to your customers for your events.

Manage Staff Logins and Roles

  • Create personalized logins for your staff members
  • Assign roles to your staff members to restrict their portal access
  • Every Staff member of your crew will have their personalized dashboard and calendar,where they can view all their schedules with all relevant details.
  • Monitor your staff to customer interactions,ensure Customer engagement.

Notum mobile app is designed by a mom with an intentiontodevelop a platform for all business interactions. We had two objectives while building the Notum Mobile app, Your privacy,andyour convenience.

Your Privacy-You are Right

We believe in this age where all the major platforms are pryingfor your personal information,there is a need for a platform which safeguards your privacy. We at Notum are dedicatedto making sure Notum is a safe, private,and secure place for you to connect with the Businesswho matter.

  • When you subscribe to a business in the Notum platform,you have the absolute authority to decide what personal information you wanted to share with that Business. No dataother than the one that you chose to share will be shared with the Business. For,e.g.,If you choose to share only your first name and last name to a business, Notum will share only that information to them. When the Businesssends a message,we will deliver the message to you based on your communication preference set in Notum.
  • We will not track you or sell your personalized information to any third-party business.
  • You have complete control over which Businesscan communicate with you. We provide a single click unsubscribe with which we guarantee that you don’t get any unwanted messages from any of the businesses.
    For,e.g.,you often end up getting lots of unwanted emails from lots of Businessthat you had previously interacted with. Even after hitting that unsubscribe button a million times,you still receive those emails over and over again.

Notum mobile app is your “one-stop solution” where you can view and respond to all the communications that are sent by the businesses who use the Notum platform.

All communications from all the businesses you interact with in one place....

  • Message prioritization assists you not to miss any high priority messages from business.

Custom Call-to Actin buttons on Messages

Tag a business to a family member

  • Quick overview of the schedules of a specific family member without the hassle of going through various messages.

Chat with businesses

  • Easily connect with your favorite businesses to make a reservation, get customer support, find deals,and more.
